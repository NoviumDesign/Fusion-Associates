Executive Search

Executive Search is not a 'buzzword' to us - Our substantial Partner experience of over 70 years fused with our search methodology & strength of our global reach deliver positive results to often complex recruitment projects.

Most of our work falls into the following categories:

‘Business-Critical’ appointments
Candidate hires critical to your business success – from middle management through to Board Level, we understand how important it is to get it right first time !

Confidential Assignments
Discreet Searches with strategic importance or to replace existing staff with ultimate discretion.  

Succession / growth
We assist you with identifying suitable talent to grow your team and/or plan ahead for future anticipated movements within the business. 
Talent Pipeline
We can create a solution to continuously identify an on-going 'pipeline' of candidates within specific disciplines in order to fuel growth and/or succession plans.
