Specialist sectors

Fusion Associates has specialist knowledge & expertise within the fashion, luxury, outdoor & sportswear sectors. 

Since the company launched in 1998 we have been supporting brands & retailers grow and adapt within an ever changing market. 

Over the years we have been key partners to our clients assisting them across multiple disciplines, hiring teams for their wholesale, retail and eCommerce channels, opening country offices, inserting design & product teams, and appointing leadership teams.
