Alex Pescott

Alex’s career in recruitment began in 1993 when he joined one of Europe’s most established fashion recruitment firms MWA to assist in the launch of a new Contracts division. 

In 1998 Alex founded Fusion Consulting and in 2009 he changed the name to Fusion Associates to reflect the new management structure of the business. 

Alex has opened & led our offices in New York, London, Stockholm, Berlin and Milan and travelled extensively throughout the USA, Europe, Middle East and Asia, networking as he went.
