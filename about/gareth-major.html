Gareth Major

Gareth joined Fusion Associates in 2005 to spearhead our expansion into Northern Europe by opening the Scandinavian office in Stockholm. 

A graduate of the London College of Fashion, Gareth’s early career saw him work in product and buying roles for a couple of the UK’s leading department store groups. 

Later he moved into the B2B and B2C trade show industry, launching the GQ consumer event and then working for Magic, the worlds largest fashion trade event to launch their European office. 

These roles have built his knowledge bank of product, process and an insight into the commercial strategies of the international brands so essential to understanding the challenges facing Fusion’s clients.
