About us

Fusion Associates is an international Executive Search firm operating exclusively within the fashion, luxury, outdoor and performance sportswear sectors.

Since our launch in the spring of 1998 we have provided global reach recruitment services & consultancy to brands and retailers across Europe, Asia and the USA.

We have combined our international industry knowledge with Executive Search methodology to assist our clients with their 'business critical' appointments, succession planning, growth strategies, brand launches, digital and international retail expansion.

Fusion Associates is based in London and led by the founder Alex Pescott, Grishma Jashapara and the team .
